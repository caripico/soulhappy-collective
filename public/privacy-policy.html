Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Who we are

Soulhappy Collective is a small independent stationery brand selling greeting cards and related products. When we refer to "we", "us", or "our", we mean Soulhappy Collective.

2. What information we collect

When you place an order or contact us, we may collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Shipping address and contact details
  • Payment information (processed securely via Stripe — we never store card details)
  • Order history

If you subscribe to our emails, we collect your email address only.

3. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfil your orders
  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • Respond to your enquiries
  • Send marketing emails if you have opted in (you can unsubscribe at any time)

4. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share it with trusted third parties as necessary to operate our business, such as payment processors (Stripe) and shipping carriers.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies to make our website function properly. See our Cookie Preferences page for more details.

6. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us via our Contact page.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please get in touch.
