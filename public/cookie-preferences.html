Cookie Preferences

Last updated: April 2025

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit to make your experience better.

How we use cookies

Soulhappy Collective uses only essential cookies necessary for the website to function. We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies.

Essential cookies

These cookies are required for the website to work and cannot be switched off. They include:

  • Cart storage — We use your browser's localStorage to remember items you've added to your cart during your session. This data stays on your device and is not sent to any server.

Third-party cookies

When you proceed to checkout, you will be directed to Stripe, our payment processor. Stripe may set its own cookies in accordance with their privacy policy.

Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of the website. For instructions, refer to your browser's help documentation.

Questions?

If you have any questions about how we use cookies, please contact us.
